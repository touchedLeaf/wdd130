Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide an online presence for the Whitewater Rafting business, showcasing various rafting trips, company information, and providing a way for customers to contact the business.

Audience

The target audience for this website includes adventure enthusiasts, families looking for vacation activities, and corporate groups seeking team-building activities.
